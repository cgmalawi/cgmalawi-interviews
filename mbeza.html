MJB: When did you get started with digital art in general?
Mbeza: I begun in December 2009 but got my first paying project in February 2010. I did a party poster for Mk. 3,150
MJB: What or when was your first actual professional job that you got paid for? How did that moment feel?
Mbeza: I did a design internship in 2010 but my first professional job was in 2011, a year before I graduated. They even offered me a better deal than I was asking for. Felt very exciting & it was unbelievable. I was finally making a living out of what I love to do.
[[Latest professional projects]]
MJB: What are you working on now? Got any epic goals in mind for your business?
Mbeza: Well am currently working for an Agency in Blantyre and working on big brands in the country. As for business, contrary to popular believe I would like to concentrate on creative businesses that are complemented by my Design skills and not limit myself to starting a design company to join the advertising market in the country but don't worry some day I will.
MJB: Do you have your own free time to train your skills or do you improve while you work?
Mbeza: I learnt the basics in early 2010 but since then I learnt most of what i know on the job and freelance. As Richard Bradson says, when someone gives you an opportunity to work one something, say yes, even though you don't know much then learn on the job.
